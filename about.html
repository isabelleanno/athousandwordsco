Our History

Although we weren't founded until 2002, our true roots go back much further. We have always been black and white.

In 1924, eight years before the color camera was invented, a young woman by the name of Betsy Smith picked up a camera for the first time- a now ancient model of camera called a "Leica 1." Thereafter, she fell in love with the newly emerging art of photography and spent her days capturing the local flora and fauna. Long story short, photography became a part of her bloodine. She inspired her kids, who inspired my Grandma, Bethany Wolowitz, to open a black and white photography store. We grew bigger, changed the name, moved to New York City, and now here we are- hiring only the best photographers in our area to capture beautiful moments across the globe.
